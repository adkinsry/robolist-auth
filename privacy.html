RoboList Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

RoboList is an Android app that helps you create and publish resale listings. This policy explains how it handles your information.

Data stored on your device

Your listings, tracked items, photos, preferences, and API credentials are stored locally on your device. RoboList does not operate a server that collects or stores your personal data, and it does not sell your data to anyone.

Third-party services you connect

When you choose to use them, RoboList sends data directly from your device to the services you configure:

  • AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google): the photos and text you submit for analysis, using your own API key.
  • eBay: when you connect your eBay account, RoboList uses your own eBay developer credentials and OAuth token to create and manage your listings on your behalf. Your eBay tokens are stored on your device, never on a server.

Each of those services processes data under its own privacy policy.

The eBay sign-in redirect

After you grant eBay permission, eBay redirects your browser to a small RoboList page that simply forwards the one-time authorization code back into the app. That page stores nothing.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the app's maintainer via the project's GitHub repository.
